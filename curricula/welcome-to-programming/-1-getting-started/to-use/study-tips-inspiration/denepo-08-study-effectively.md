# Study Effectively

> __Studying__: Managing your limited resources to maximize learning.

Resources that can run out: Time, motivation, concentration, social support, thinking power, energy.

## Know Yourself
There is no single best way to study. The first step is to know your own situation and limitations.

## Some Strategies

TL;DR:
* Always remember why you're doing this.
* Have a clear vision of the skills you want to gain.
* Keep your study sessions short, fun, and regular.
* Organize your studying around a carefully constructed set of questions.
* Focus on one new concept at a time, and learn it in its real-world context.
* Mix it up. Alternate regularly between different tasks.
* Help your friends whenever they ask. You'll both learn more this way.

By resource:
* **Time**: Organize sessions around well-formulated questions. Learn to cut your losses. Collect good resources ahead of time.
* **Motivation**: Don't lose sight of why. Keep studying light and fun. Keep a daily learning journal.
* **Working Memory**: Keep it clear using cognitive tools. Don't multi-task. Practice intentional thinking.
* **Cognitive Load**: Keep study space distraction-free. Follow Read, Diagram, Modify, Create progression. Make frequent connections to existing knowledge.
* **Social Support**: Teach others. Rely on others. Respect others' needs.
* **Concentration**: Take breaks. Learn about Diffused Thinking. Keep a short study plan visible.
